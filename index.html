ABOUT US

Welcome to the official website of the Computer Science Club at Utah Valley University! After a brief period of inactivity, the club is up and running again thanks to the current club president, Clayton Oaks. We currently get together every Monday at 7:00 pm in Room CS 404 at Utah Valley University. Feel free to come to any of our meetings where you could get the chance to participate in one of our workshops, project/startup pitches, networking/LinkedIn/recruiting events, or listen to one of our guest speakers!

Our goal is you.

We hope to provide you with the opportunities that simply aren't available during class hours.

EVENTS

Torick D.

Guest Speaker - Apr 11, 2022

We are excited to be hosting Torick D., who is currently a Software Engineer at VIA Technologies! Torick will be setting up a programming challenge/competition for everyone who attends the event. The Computer Science club is also happy to be providing pizza and beverages for all those who attend. Feel free to stop by and enjoy the fun, even if it's only for a few minutes!

CA Engineering

Internships/Jobs - Mar 21, 2022

We have been given the opportunity to host representatives from CA Engineering! They will be discussing what kind of work is done at CA Engineering as well as internship and job opportunities. They will also be providing food on a first come first serve basis. Don't miss out on this excellent opportunity to land an internship or job!

Krystal Mitchell

Guest Speaker - Dec 6, 2021

We will be hosting Senior Software Engineer Krystal Mitchell for a Q&A! She works on increasing growth and revenue at New Relic. Krystal graduated from UVU with a degree in UX Design & Development, with an emphasis in Web and App Development. She will be answering all your questions on topics from technical interviews to user experience.

Podium

Company Tour - Nov 9, 2021

Join us on our visit to Podium in Lehi, Utah! If you don't know about Podium, they make messaging tools for businesses. They raised a $125MM Series C last year, led by Y Combinator, valuing them at $1.5B. We will be touring one of the two facilities on site. The meeting will be on Tuesday instead of Monday.

A welcoming environment.

We encourage all concentrations of computer science and related fields — cybersecurity, information technology, software engineering, and more — to join!
